🚀 Quick Start:
1. Wait for all pods to be ready:
   kubectl get pods -l app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "vista3d.name" . }}

{{- if not .Values.ingress.enabled }}
2. Port forward to access the services:
   # Frontend (Streamlit)
   kubectl port-forward service/{{ .Values.frontend.name }} 8501:8501
   
   # Image Server (FastAPI)
   kubectl port-forward service/{{ .Values.imageServer.name }} 8888:8888
{{- end }}

3. Open your browser and navigate to http://localhost:8501

4. Explore the sample data or upload your own medical imaging data
